ColorBlindify

Privacy Policy

1. Overview

ColorBlindify respects your privacy. This extension is designed to provide color vision testing and correction with zero data collection. All processing happens locally on your device.

2. Data Collection

ColorBlindify does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. This includes:

  • No personal identification information
  • No browsing history
  • No health or medical data
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No IP addresses or location data

3. Local Storage

The extension only uses Chrome's local sync storage to save your preferences:

  • Selected color correction mode (protanopia, deuteranopia, tritanopia, or normal)
  • Filter type (simulate or correct)
  • Intensity/severity setting (0.0 - 1.0)

These settings remain on your device and are synchronized across your Chrome profile only.

4. Permissions

ColorBlindify requests only the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save and load your color filter preferences
  • Host access (<all_urls>): To apply color corrections to webpages you visit

These permissions are necessary for core functionality. No additional data collection occurs.

5. How It Works

ColorBlindify operates entirely in your browser:

  1. You take a built-in color vision test
  2. The algorithm analyzes your responses locally
  3. Results generate a personalized severity score
  4. You choose a filter and adjust intensity
  5. Filters apply to webpages using CSS color matrices

All computation happens on your device. No data leaves your computer.

6. Third Parties

ColorBlindify does not integrate with third-party services, analytics platforms, or ad networks. Your data is never shared with external parties.

7. Security

Since ColorBlindify does not collect data, there is no data to compromise. Your settings are stored securely in Chrome's encrypted sync storage, protected by your Google account security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as the extension evolves. We encourage you to review it periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated clearly.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how ColorBlindify handles your data, you can reach out through the Chrome Web Store extension page.

ColorBlindify is committed to building accessible tools that respect user privacy.
